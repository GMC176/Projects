Bhandardara is a beautiful hill station located in the Ahmednagar district of Maharashtra, India. It is known for its scenic landscapes, lush greenery, serene lakes, and majestic waterfalls. Bhandardara is famous for the picturesque Arthur Lake, also known as Bhandardara Lake. During the monsoon season, Bhandardara is adorned with several small seasonal waterfalls, including the Umbrella Falls. It is a reservoir formed by the Wilson Dam on the Pravara River. The tranquil lake surrounded by hills offers stunning views and boating opportunities.
Address: Bhandardara, Ahmednagar, Maharashtra

gatpuri is a charming hill town located in the Nashik district of Maharashtra, India. It is known for its natural beauty, picturesque landscapes, and pleasant climate.
Igatpuri is surrounded by lush green hills, dense forests, and scenic valleys, offering breathtaking views and a serene atmosphere. The town is particularly enchanting during the monsoon season when the hills are covered with mist and the waterfalls are in full flow.
Igatpuri is home to the Vipassana International Academy, one of the world's largest meditation centers. Igatpuri is a paradise for nature enthusiasts and trekkers. It offers several trekking trails, including the popular Kalsubai Peak, which is the highest peak in Maharashtra. The trails take you through lush forests, streams, and scenic viewpoints.: The Bhatsa River Valley near Igatpuri is a picturesque location with beautiful landscapes and the serene Bhatsa River flowing through it.
Address: Igatpuri, Nashik District, Maharashtra

The Nandurbar Bird Sanctuary, is a prominent bird sanctuary located in the Nandurbar district of Maharashtra.: The sanctuary is home to a diverse range of bird species, both resident and migratory. Over 230 bird species have been recorded in the sanctuary, including flamingos, storks, herons, cranes, ibises, and several species of ducks and waders.
The Nandurbar Bird Sanctuary is a popular destination for birdwatchers and nature enthusiasts. Visitors can indulge in bird watching activities, observing the diverse bird species in their natural habitat. The sanctuary offers excellent opportunities for bird photography as well.
the sanctuary is also home to a variety of flora and fauna. The wetland ecosystem supports aquatic plants, reeds, and grasses.
Address: Nandurbar, Maharashtra
