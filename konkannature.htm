Tarkarli Beach is known for its pristine white sand, crystal-clear turquoise waters, and picturesque surroundings.Tarkarli Beach is known for its natural beauty and tranquility.
Tarkarli Beach is known for its natural beauty and tranquility. The beach offers a peaceful and serene atmosphere with clean shores and relatively fewer crowds compared to some other popular beaches in the region. The clear waters of the Arabian Sea make it an ideal spot for swimming, snorkeling, and scuba diving.Tarkarli Beach is famous for its water sports activities. Visitors can indulge in thrilling experiences such as snorkeling, scuba diving, banana boat rides, jet skiing, and parasailing
Address: Tarkarli, Malvan, Sindhudurg District, Maharashtra

Kashid Beach is a beautiful coastal destination situated in the Raigad district of Maharashtra, India. It is known for its long stretch of white sand, clear turquoise waters, and picturesque surroundings. Kashid Beach is renowned for its natural beauty and tranquil atmosphere.
The beach is characterized by its pristine white sand and clean shores. The crystal-clear waters of the Arabian Sea make it an ideal spot for swimming, sunbathing, and water sports. Kashid Beach offers various water sports activities for adventure enthusiasts.
Visitors can enjoy thrilling experiences such as jet skiing, banana boat rides, parasailing, and kayaking.The beach is surrounded by lush green hills, coconut groves, and casuarina trees, adding to its scenic charm. The picturesque landscape offers a tranquil setting for leisurely walks, picnics, and enjoying the natural beauty of the surroundings. Kashid Beach is located in close proximity to several tourist attractions. One of the prominent attractions is the Korlai Fort, an ancient Portuguese fort built during the 16th century.
Address: Kashid Village, Murud Taluka, Raigad District, Maharashtra

Karnala Bird Sanctuary, located near Panvel in the Raigad district of Maharashtra, India, is a popular destination for bird enthusiasts and nature lovers.The sanctuary covers an area of around 4.8 square kilometers and is known for its rich biodiversity.
It is home to more than 150 species of resident and migratory birds, making it a haven for birdwatching enthusiasts. Some of the notable bird species found here include the Malabar Whistling Thrush, Ashy Minivet, Malabar Grey Hornbill, and the rare Rufous-tailed Lark.Apart from the diverse birdlife, Karnala Bird Sanctuary is also home to a variety of plant and animal species.
The forested area of the sanctuary consists of a mix of moist deciduous and semi-evergreen forests. Visitors can spot various mammals, reptiles, butterflies, and insects while exploring the sanctuary.
The sanctuary offers several trails and pathways for visitors to explore and observe the avian species.There is also a nature interpretation center where visitors can learn more about the flora, fauna, and bird species found in the sanctuary.
Address: Karnala, Panvel, Raigad District, Maharashtra

Koyna Wildlife Sanctuary is a protected area located in the Satara district of Maharashtra, India. It is situated in the Western Ghats region and is known for its rich biodiversity and scenic beauty.
The sanctuary is known for its diverse flora and fauna. It is covered with dense forests comprising various tree species, including teak, shisham, ain, bamboo, and many more.The sanctuary is home to a variety of wildlife, including mammals like Indian bison (gaur), sloth bear, sambar deer, leopard, and several species of reptiles, amphibians, and birds.
Koyna Wildlife Sanctuary offers trekking and nature trails for visitors to explore the natural beauty of the region.The trails pass through dense forests and offer breathtaking views of the surrounding hills, valleys, and water bodies. Trekking enthusiasts can enjoy the adventure and experience the wilderness of the sanctuary.
Address: Western Ghats, Ratnagiri and Satara districts, Maharashtra
