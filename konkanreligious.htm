Ganpatipule is a small coastal town located in the Ratnagiri district of Maharashtra, India. It is known for its scenic beauty, pristine beaches, and the famous Ganpatipule Temple, which is dedicated to Lord Ganesha.
The Ganpatipule Temple is the main attraction of the town. It houses a self-manifested idol of Lord Ganesha, which is believed to be about 400 years old. The temple is situated on a hillside, overlooking the Arabian Sea. The idol of Lord Ganesha is made of copper and stands at a height of around 6 meters.The idol of Lord Ganesha at Ganpatipule Temple is considered to be a Swayambhu, meaning it is a naturally formed or self-manifested idol. It is believed to have emerged from the soil.
Address: Ganpatipule, Ratnagiri District, Maharashtra

Vajreshwari Temple is a renowned Hindu temple located in the town of Vajreshwari, which is situated in the Bhiwandi taluka of Thane district in Maharashtra, India. The temple is dedicated to Goddess Vajreshwari, who is considered an incarnation of Goddess Parvati.
The temple is nestled in the picturesque surroundings of the Sahyadri Mountains and is known for its spiritual significance and architectural beauty. It attracts a large number of devotees and visitors throughout the year.
Vajreshwari is also famous for its hot springs, known as "Akloli Kund," which are believed to have medicinal properties.Apart from the main deity, the temple complex also houses several other shrines dedicated to various deities, including Lord Shiva, Lord Ganesha, and Lord Hanuman.Many visitors come to the temple to take a dip in these hot springs, as it is believed to have healing effects on various ailments.
Address: Vajreshwari Road, Vajreshwari, Bhiwandi, Thane District, Maharashtra,

Harihareshwar is a coastal town located in the Raigad district of Maharashtra, India. It is a popular tourist destination known for its beautiful beaches, ancient temples, and serene natural surroundings. The main attraction of Harihareshwar is the Harihareshwar Temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva.
The temple complex consists of two main shrines, one housing the idols of Lord Hari (Vishnu) and Lord Hara (Shiva). The temple is situated on a hill overlooking the Arabian Sea and offers breathtaking views of the surrounding area.
Another significant temple in Harihareshwar is the Kalbhairav Temple. It is dedicated to Lord Kalbhairav, an incarnation of Lord Shiva. The temple is known for its ancient architecture and religious significance.
Ganesh Gully is a narrow and rocky passage near Harihareshwar Beach. It is named after the resemblance of the passage to the trunk of Lord Ganesha. Visitors can explore this unique geological formation and enjoy the natural beauty of the surroundings.
Address: Harihareshwar, Raigad District,Maharashtra

Kunkeshwar is a coastal village located in the Sindhudurg district of Maharashtra, India. It is famous for its ancient Kunkeshwar Temple dedicated to Lord Shiva and its picturesque beach. The main attraction of Kunkeshwar is the Kunkeshwar Temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva. The temple is believed to have been built in the 12th century and showcases beautiful architectural craftsmanship. The sanctum of the temple houses a Shiva Linga (a representation of Lord Shiva) and is considered highly sacred by devotees.
Adjacent to the temple, there is a lighthouse that adds to the charm of Kunkeshwar. Visitors can climb the lighthouse to enjoy panoramic views of the coastline and the surrounding areas. The lighthouse offers a unique perspective and is a great spot for photography enthusiasts.
Kunkeshwar is known for its unspoiled natural beauty. The village is surrounded by lush green hills and offers a serene atmosphere. Visitors can explore the nearby areas, take nature walks, and enjoy the tranquility of the surroundings.
Address: Kunkeshwar, Sindhudurg, Maharashtra
