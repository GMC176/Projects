Konkan Tourism

Konkan is a great place for Tourism, traveling at Konkan can bring pleasure and adventure in your life. Explore Konkan’s coastal experiences, waterfalls, nature, forts, and delicious foods.Konkan, a beautiful place which is as good as a paradise, a coastline at the western part of India.
The sandy golden beaches with crystal clear ocean are the main attractions for all the travelers across the world to visit Konkan at least once in a lifetime. Konkan beaches are not typical beaches which are most crowded. You will find peace and relaxation on many of the Konkan beaches.But Konkan is just not all about the beaches. Konkan Tourism has many more attributes that everyone ought to know about.

While traveling by Konkan railway, you are certainly going to be very impressed by the mountain ranges and green carpet of trees and villages. Konkan railway passes through some breathtaking modern tunnels and bridges, which are also major highlights when you travel by Konkan railway.You could see a small village’s pops out in the valleys, which greet each other well with the natures.
Apart from Konkan hill ranges, you will be happy to see green rice fields everywhere due to heavy rainfalls in the Konkan region. During the rainy season, the main attractions are huge waterfalls at Ghats. From milky and crystal waterfalls, which will stun you with the coolest environment.

The best time to visit Konkan is during monsoon and winter season. But if you are visiting Konkan during the summer season. You can spend quality time at Konkan beaches and enjoy seafood cuisines. Tarkarli beach is one of the famous beaches.
Konkan Tourism is just not about visiting the places but also understand the lifestyle of Konkan people. Konkan people live a very simple life and love the exotic gift which they got from the god. Alphonso mango is a famous mango in India. Alphonso mango which is known as “hapus” is the king of mangoes. Alphonso mangoes are exported in any part of Indian and abroad because of its huge demand in the market every year.You will probably see lots of mango orchards farms everywhere.
Also, you should not forget the opportunity of drinking fresh coconut water in Konkan. Coconut fields are found everywhere in Konkan regions.
