Monuments And Forts

Sindhudurg Fort is a historic fort located in the coastal town of Malvan in the Sindhudurg district of Maharashtra, India. It was built by the Maratha king, Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, in the 17th century.The fort was strategically built to protect the Konkan coastline from invasions and to serve as a naval base for the Maratha Navy.
Sindhudurg Fort played a crucial role in protecting the Konkan region from foreign invasions, particularly from European colonial powers. It served as a base for the Maratha Navy and played a vital role in maritime activities and trade.The fort has several notable structures within its premises. These include the main entrance gate called the Dilli Darwaza, the Jaroka Darwaza, the Kalal Bangadi
Address: Malvan, Sindhudurg District, Maharashtra

Vijaydurg Fort, also known as Victory Fort, is a historic fort located in the Devgad taluka of the Sindhudurg district.Vijaydurg Fort was built during the reign of the Maratha Empire under the leadership of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj in the 17th century.It was strategically constructed on a rocky outcrop along the Arabian Sea coast to serve as a defense stronghold.
The fort boasts several notable features. It has a well-preserved entrance gate called "Mahadarwaja" that showcases the Maratha architectural style. The fortifications are fortified with bastions and watchtowers, providing panoramic views of the Arabian Sea. The fort also houses various temples, including the Goddess Shivai Temple and Kanhoji Angre Samadhi (memorial).
Within the fort premises, there is a Naval Heritage Museum that exhibits artifacts, documents, and models showcasing the maritime history of the region. The museum provides insights into the naval prowess of the Marathas and their contributions to the maritime trade.
Address: Devgad Taluka, Sindhudurg District, Maharashtra

Raigad Fort, also known as the King of Forts, is a significant historical fortress located in the Raigad district of Maharashtra, India. It holds immense historical and cultural importance as it served as the capital of the Maratha Empire under the rule of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj.The fort showcases the military architecture of the Marathas. It has a unique layout with multiple levels and sections, including the Mena Darwaja (main entrance), Nagarkhana Darwaja (drum house entrance), the queen's quarters, the king's court, and the grand Palkhi Darwaja.
One of the popular ways to reach the fort is through the Raigad Ropeway, a cable car system that takes visitors from the base to the top of the fort. It offers a scenic and convenient way to access the fort while enjoying breathtaking views of the surroundings.The main attractions include the Raj Bhavan (royal residence), the Takmak Tok (execution point), Jagadishwar Temple, and the Gangasagar Lake, among others. The Samadhi (memorial) of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj and his loyal dog Waghya is also located within the fort complex.
Address: Mahad, Raigad District, Maharashtra

Janjira Fort, also known as Murud-Janjira Fort, is a renowned historical fortress located on an island off the coastal town of Murud in the Raigad district of Maharashtra, India. It is famous for its architectural splendor and strategic location.
Inside the fort, there are several structures of historical and cultural significance. These include the palace of the Nawabs (rulers), residential areas for soldiers, a freshwater tank called Ganga Sagar Lake, a mosque, and a temple. Despite the passage of time, some structures within the fort complex remain intact, giving a glimpse into the grandeur of the past.
Janjira Fort offers breathtaking views of the Arabian Sea and the surrounding landscapes. Visitors can enjoy panoramic vistas of the ocean, nearby beaches, and the picturesque town of Murud from the fort's vantage points.
Address: Murud, Raigad District, Maharashtra
